About Me

My name is Ruba, and I’m a passionate graphic designer with a creative eye for detail.

I’m a graphic designer who enjoys bringing imagination to life through design. Whether it’s creating logos, posters, or digital art, I love experimenting with colors, layouts, and styles. Designing isn’t just my profession — it’s my way of expressing creativity.

This allowed me to specialize in storytelling, using skills like branding, design, motion, to drive the user’s attention to the right places and create more engaging and memorable experiences.
